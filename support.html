Support

Welcome! This page has answers to common questions and troubleshooting tips for Straunts.

FAQ

How does sync work?

Your restaurants, ratings, and notes sync across your devices via iCloud automatically. Each device signed in to the same iCloud account will stay in sync.

Note: Individual visit details like companions, dishes, and photos don't sync yet — use the Export Backup feature to transfer those to a new device.

How do I back up my data?

Go to Settings > Data & Backup > Export Backup. This creates a JSON file with everything — restaurants, ratings, notes, tags, visits, companions, dishes, and photos. Save it anywhere and restore it anytime from the same menu.

Can I use this on multiple devices?

Yes! Sign in with the same iCloud account on each device and your restaurants and ratings will sync automatically. Use Export Backup to move visit details (dishes, companions, photos) between devices.

What if something looks out of sync?

Go to Settings > Data & Backup and tap "Sync Now" to manually refresh. If you're still not seeing changes, make sure you're signed in to iCloud in Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud.

Can I add photos to my restaurants?

Photos aren't available yet, but it's on the roadmap! The app currently lets you track visits, rate dishes, and take notes about your experience.

Troubleshooting

iCloud sync isn't working

  • Make sure you're signed in to iCloud (Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud)
  • Check that you have a stable internet connection
  • Try tapping "Sync Now" in Settings > Data & Backup
  • Force-close the app and reopen it

I restored a backup and lost data

Restore merges with existing data — it doesn't replace it. If you restored the wrong backup, restore the correct one again to merge it back in.

The app crashed

  1. Force-close the app and reopen it
  2. If it keeps crashing, try exporting a backup (Settings > Data & Backup > Export Backup)
  3. Force-close again and restore the backup (Settings > Data & Backup > Restore from Backup)
  4. If that doesn't help, reach out with details (see Contact Us below)

A restaurant isn't showing up

  • Try scrolling down to see if it's below the fold
  • Use the search bar to find it by name
  • Try tapping "Sync Now" in Settings if you recently added it on another device

Export Backup shows as disabled

The Export Backup button is disabled when you have no restaurants saved. Add a restaurant first and try again.

Privacy & Data

Your data is yours. Everything is stored locally on your device and synced privately via iCloud. We don't have access to your data, and we don't collect analytics or usage information.

When you export a backup, the file is saved to your device and shared only if you choose to share it.
