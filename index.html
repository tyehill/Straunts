Straunts

Track the restaurants you love. Remember every meal.

Straunts is a simple, private way to keep track of the restaurants you visit, rate your dishes, and jot down notes so you never forget what to order next time.

Track Visits

Log every restaurant you try and keep a running history of your visits.

Rate & Take Notes

Rate dishes and add notes so your favorites — and the ones to skip — are easy to remember.

Sync Across Devices

Your restaurants, ratings, and notes sync automatically via iCloud on every device you own.

Export & Back Up

Export a full backup anytime — restaurants, ratings, notes, visits, and more — in a single file.

Privacy first

Straunts doesn't collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything lives on your device and syncs privately through your own iCloud account — we never see it.
